NARRATIVE:
Officer Nair was dispatched at 05:45 AM following a report from a building
security guard who discovered extensive graffiti on the east wall of 500
Crawford Street. The graffiti covered approximately 8 by 12 feet of the
wall surface. Multiple spray paint colors were used: red, black, and silver.

Two distinct tag signatures are visible and consistent with known graffiti
crews active in the downtown corridor. Similar tags have appeared at three
other locations on Crawford and Main Streets this month.

Damage is estimated at $1,200 for professional removal and repainting.

EVIDENCE COLLECTED:
1. Photographs of all graffiti (4 photos, attached to case file)
2. Aerosol spray paint residue — cap style consistent with Montana brand cans
3. No CCTV camera coverage of east wall at time of incident

SUSPECT DESCRIPTION:
Unknown. Tag signatures suggest a crew of 2–3 individuals based on
simultaneous tag placement at multiple heights on the wall.

DISPOSITION:
Case open. Photos forwarded to HPD Gang/Graffiti Unit for tag identification.
Building owner provided case number for insurance claim.
