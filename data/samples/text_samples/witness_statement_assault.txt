STATEMENT:

On the night of November 20th, 2024, at approximately 11:45 PM, I was
walking home from dinner along Westheimer Road when I witnessed a violent
incident outside a nightclub called Club Luxe at the intersection of
Westheimer and Montrose.

Two males were attacking a woman near the bus stop on Westheimer Road.
The first male was wearing a bright red jacket, approximately 6 feet tall,
with a medium build and short black hair. The second male was shorter,
approximately 5 feet 8 inches, wearing a white hoodie with the hood down.

The woman was screaming for help. The male in the red jacket grabbed her
black leather purse — it had a gold clasp on the front — while the male in
the white hoodie held her by the arms. She fell to the ground when the
strap broke. Both suspects ran east toward the parking garage on
Montrose Boulevard immediately after taking the purse.

I called 911 immediately from my cell phone. The entire incident lasted
approximately 30 seconds. I stayed at the scene and spoke with Officers
Reeves and Torres when they arrived. I am willing to cooperate fully and
identify the suspects in a lineup if necessary.

— Robert Chen

Signed in the presence of Officer D. Reeves, HPD District 3.
